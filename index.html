What we do

Eames Institute

Ray and Charles Eames laid the foundation for modern design
and created work that has transcended time. With an
aspiration to bring the Eameses’ timeless methodologies to a
modern audience, the Eames Institute enlisted Instrument to
co-create a digital platform to unveil their vast collection to the world.

Dropbox "For All Things Worth Saving"

To evolve and mature how Dropbox engages with their
customers, we developed a multi-year brand platform as well as
launched its first campaign titled 'For All Things Worth
Saving' — an ecosystem of branded experiences aimed to
remind people of the inherent value of their digital files.
